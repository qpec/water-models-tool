## Other measured / policy values
- MTR/MTW-oppervlaktewater norm total-P = 0.15 mg P/l (frequently exceeded in drainwater)
- Drain-water P: some fields 0.3-0.8 mg/l, others 3-7.5 mg/l; deeper layers (drains) 3-8 mg/l P
- Total-N drainwater 4-9 mg/l (norm cited 2.2 mg/l) [edepot_650777]
- Hogeveensepolder summer-mean P: 7.20 mg/l (2001), recent ~4 mg/l (>MTR)
- Bollenstreek drainage split: 30% open drain->sloot, 15% closed, 50% bronbemaling, 5% combi
- Neerslagoverschot assumed 35 cm/yr; drain spacing 10 m (grof zand) / 8 m (fijn zand)
